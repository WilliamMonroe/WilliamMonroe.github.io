*********************************************** UNIFIED OPERATING SYSTEM BUILD 2.3.7 -- 10.15.2077 ***********************************************

Clay Lockwood was born in Vault 107 which had an experiment consisting on studying the practical implications of a long term period of eugenics in
a population. At birth new dwellers in the Vault underwent phsyical and genetic tests to start a "Life Profile" that would be built up over their
life time, providing the Vault's ZAX Mainframe with information to decide what couples may be permitted to reproduce with the goal of improving
the Gene Pool in the Vault over succeeding generations.
It's common practice in Vault 107 for babies born with physical deformities to be euthanized immedately after birth. At the age of twelve Vault
Dwellers go undergo mental examinantion and testing to identify any "deficiencies" which impact the score of their profile. Children with these
phsyical or mental conditions as well as children that were not born in a "sanctioned birth" are often called "runts" by the rest of the Vault
and face a life of shaming until their death or their exiling from the Vault. Clay was one of these such children, he was disowned by his family
and exiled from the Vault a single day before his fifteenth birthday.
